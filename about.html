About me

My name is Caleb Besser, I was born December 1, 1998. I am a Chico State Graduate, with a Bachelors of Science, in Computer Science. I enjoy the problem solving side of programming, figuring out the root of a problem and finding the answer from there. I have experience in many different languages, such as C, C#, C++, OCaml, Python, Javascript, and many more. But my main and favorite language is most likely C#, it and C are where I have spent a majority of my time.

I currently am seeking employment in the Software Engineering field, and specifically Front/Backend Web/App Development.
